Generation E - from South to North

The arrows show the migration movements of Southern Europeans over the last years. The thicker the arrows, the larger the number of migrants.

The data is not representative, since it was collected with the questionnaire of the project Generation E. Please help us to get more data and stories on generatione.eu.
